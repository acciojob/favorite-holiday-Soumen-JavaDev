Top 5 Attractions in Goa

  1. Baga Beach – One of Goa's most famous beaches, known for its water sports and vibrant nightlife.
  2. Fort Aguada – A 17th-century Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
  3. Dudhsagar Falls – A spectacular four-tiered waterfall, surrounded by lush greenery.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A UNESCO World Heritage site that houses the relics of St. Francis Xavier.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market – A bustling market where you can buy souvenirs, clothes, and handcrafted items.

Must-Try Foods in Goa

  • Goan Fish Curry – A tangy and spicy fish curry made with coconut, tamarind, and local spices.
  • Prawn Balchão – A fiery Goan dish where prawns are cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce.
  • Vindaloo – A popular dish with Portuguese roots, made with marinated pork and spicy red chili paste.
  • Bebinca – A traditional Goan dessert made of layered coconut milk and flour.
  • Sorpotel – A rich meat curry made with pork and a variety of spices, typically served during celebrations.
